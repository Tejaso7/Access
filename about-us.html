About Access Mail

During a tough recession, I faced constant rejections despite applying to numerous jobs. Frustrated by the lack of callbacks, I decided to take a different approach. I researched and extracted emails of founders and co-founders of startup companies, bypassing traditional hiring channels. This simple shift led to multiple interview opportunities, and that’s when I realized the power of direct outreach.

With Accessmails, I’m offering the same approach—helping others connect directly with decision-makers and secure interviews faster.
