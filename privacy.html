Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Access Mail collects, uses, and protects your information in compliance with Indian data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000.

1. Information We Collect

We collect certain personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details when you interact with our services, particularly when:

  • You contact us via forms or email.
  • You sign up for job opportunities or other services.
  • You make payments or transactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with access to exclusive job opportunities.
  • To contact you with details about roles, company executives, and services.
  • To send you updates, newsletters, or relevant notifications (if you have opted in).
  • To respond to your queries or provide support.
  • To ensure compliance with legal obligations under Indian law.

3. Information We Do Not Provide

Access Mail does not:

  • Sell, share, or rent your personal information to third-party marketing services.
  • Disclose your contact information, unless authorized or legally required.
  • Store payment details once a transaction has been completed through our payment gateway (Razorpay).

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Legal Compliance

Access Mail complies with all applicable data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.

Your personal data is processed in accordance with these regulations and any legal requirements enforced by the Indian government.

7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Right to access: You may request details of personal information that we hold about you.
  • Right to correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate data.
  • Right to deletion: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal obligations we have to retain it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our services or legal obligations. Any changes will be posted on this page.
